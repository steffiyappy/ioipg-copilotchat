Hadar,

Confirmed for the 3 PM call. A few financial points to anchor the discussion:

1. LAD EXPOSURE: Under Clause 22 of the standard SPA, LAD is triggered only if delay exceeds the Grace Period of 30 days beyond the VP date. A 3-week delay from 30 April to 21 May = 21 days. We are currently within the Grace Period, so NO LAD is payable at this stage. However, if the delay extends beyond 30 May 2025, LAD kicks in at 10% per annum on the purchase price per affected unit. For 34 units at an average purchase price of RM 1,050,000, LAD exposure would be approximately RM 9,800 per unit per month (RM 333,200/month for all 34 units).

2. ACCELERATION COST: I've done a quick estimate on bringing in a second wireman crew. Cost to bring in Elektro Prima (pre-qualified panel contractor) for a 3-week intensive programme: approximately RM 185,000 all-in. This is significantly cheaper than LAD exposure.

3. GOODWILL GESTURE: If we want to preempt complaints and protect our brand, I'd suggest a RM 2,500–3,000 goodwill voucher for each affected buyer (redeemable against renovation, legal fees, or F&B at IOI Mall). Total cost: RM 85,000–102,000 for 34 units. I'd recommend this even if LAD isn't triggered – it's good customer relations and will likely prevent formal escalations.
